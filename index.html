Our Story

A Little Shop Full
of Big Heart

Southern Suds & Gifts is your go-to destination for unique, locally-made treasures right here in downtown Wake Forest. Our shop is filled with handcrafted goods — from artisan soaps and door hangers, to jewelry, wreaths, woven baskets, home décor, and so much more.

Every item you find here supports a local maker. We're proud to provide a space where North Carolina artists can share their craft with our wonderful community.
